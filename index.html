Featured Courses

Courses Tailored to Your Needs

Choose the program that fits your goals. From beginner modules to professional refinement courses, we have you covered.

Beginner 2 Months

Basic English Speaking

Best for those who struggle with basic grammar, hesitate to speak in public, or translate from their native tongue before speaking.

What you will learn:

  • Tenses & Basic Sentence Structure
  • Everyday Conversational Phrases
  • Fluency building & Stage fear removal
Course Highlights:

This beginner course focuses on establishing correct grammar and simple conversational structures. Includes weekly practical activities where you practice introduces yourself, shopping dialogues, and basic telephonic conversations.

Prerequisites:

Basic reading and understanding of English words are required.

Intermediate 3 Months

Intermediate Fluency Course

Best for individuals who know basic English but struggle with vocabulary, face grammatical errors, and want to improve interactive flow.

What you will learn:

  • Vocabulary Enhancing Techniques
  • Group Discussions & Debate Formats
  • Public Speaking & Extempore drills
Course Highlights:

Deep dive into conversational templates. Highlights include debating techniques, hosting presentations, participating in active group discussions (GDs), and body language grooming for confidence.

Prerequisites:

Ability to form basic sentences and speak minimal English is needed.

Advanced 3 Months

Advanced Business Communication

Best for corporate workers, leads, and job seekers aiming to present elegantly, lead client meetings, write emails, and build networks.

What you will learn:

  • Client Pitch & Sales Communication
  • Corporate Email Etiquette & Resumes
  • Grooming & Professional Etiquette
Course Highlights:

Specially curated module targeting corporate settings. Focuses on negotiating, resolving conflicts, running office presentations, drafting crisp executive emails, and preparation for rigorous mock job interviews.

Prerequisites:

Reasonable spoken English fluency is required. Ideal for working professionals.
